Art

Discover exceptional works spanning centuries of creative expression, from Old Master paintings to cutting-edge contemporary pieces. Our carefully curated art auctions feature significant works by established masters and emerging talents alike, each selected for their artistic merit, historical importance, and market relevance. Clocks

Journey through time with our exceptional horology collection, featuring precision timepieces that showcase centuries of craftsmanship and innovation. From ornate grandfather clocks with intricate complications to sleek modernist designs, each timepiece represents both artistic expression and mechanical ingenuity. Our horology experts select only the finest examples, evaluating movement quality, case design, provenance, and historical significance. These functional sculptures connect collectors to rich traditions of craftsmanship while serving as sophisticated focal points in distinguished spaces.
