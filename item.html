A sublime fusion of mathematical precision and organic form, Φ: Canis is a masterful contemporary sculpture by the esteemed artist Alistair Vennholm. Drawing inspiration from the golden ratio (Φ) and the timeless silhouette of man's most loyal companion, this piece transcends mere representation to become a study in balance, proportion, and fluidity. Carved with meticulous precision, the sculpture’s surface is adorned with rhythmic, undulating grooves, creating a mesmerizing interplay of light and shadow. Its monochromatic finish enhances its sculptural depth, allowing the form to shift dynamically with every change in perspective. At once both futuristic and primal, Φ: Canis invites contemplation on the harmony between nature and design. A rare and evocative work, this piece stands as a testament to Vennholm’s ability to merge the philosophical with the tangible—an essential acquisition for collectors of avant-garde sculpture. Height: 75 cm | Medium: Cast resin with lacquered finish | Provenance: Private collection, London
